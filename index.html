Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our app and mission

Many people in Egypt, especially in large cities, struggle with finding clear, updated, and reliable information about public transportation. There's no central platform that shows all transport options (metro, buses, minibuses, etc.) with real-time guidance or simple trip planning.
  • Students traveling to school or university
  • Tourists and new residents unfamiliar with local routes
  • People in new neighborhoods or far-out areas
  • Workers commuting between cities
  • Middle-class people who can’t justify spending 250 EGP a day — or 500 for the round trip — just to get to work or university
  • People who rely on shared/public transport but hate how chaotic it is
  • Covers all types of transport in Egypt — formal and informal
  • Suggested routes to your destination
  • Real-time traffic & crowd info (based on user data + integration with transport APIs)
  • Clean map view with stop/station names & directions
  • Premium features like early trip alerts, saved routes, and no-ad experience
  • Current apps like Google Maps don't show all informal transport options (like microbuses)
  • No existing app gives full coverage of Egypt's chaotic transport system
  • We include both government and private transport systems
  • Real-time crowdsourced feedback from users
